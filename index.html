Who we are

We are a multidisciplinary group of students, majoring in Business Administration, Computer Science and Automation Engineering (a.k.a. putos amos del universo), from the University of Alcala in Spain. We liked the idea of challenging ourselves and working on something real, so we decided to join the Robotic Day 2017 competition in Prague.

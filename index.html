The team
Utopian Robotics is a team created by students of the Alcala de Henares University (Alcala de Henares, Madrid, Spain) to compete at the Robotics Day 2017 competition on Prague.

We are five members on the team. Three of us are finishing our degree on Industrial Engineering, another one of us is finishing his degree on Informatics Engineering, and the last member is a student of the Business Management and Administration Degree.

We wish to participate at the “Roadside Assistance” competition of the Robotic Day 2017. This competition is created under the importance of quick and efficient assistance at road accidents, mainly at the importance of clearing the area to make the traffic fluid and avoid further harm. We will compete at a simplified version of side situation. You can find the specifics at their official website.

The goal is to win. But the main goal is to learn and develop new technology for the future.

¡Thank you for taken interest on our team!
